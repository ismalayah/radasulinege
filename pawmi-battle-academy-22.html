For more information on this Pokémon's species, see Pawmi.

Pawmi (Japanese: パモ Pamo) is a Lightning-type Basic Pokémon card. It is part of the Pokémon Card Game Battle Academy Deck Kit.

Card text

Targeted Spark
ねらってスパーク
This attack does 20 damage to 1 of your opponent's Pokémon. (Don't apply Weakness and Resistance for Benched Pokémon.)

Pokédex data

Trivia

Origin

Targeted Spark is an attack that first appeared on Morpeko from Fusion Strike. This card's Pokédex entry comes from Pokémon Violet.
