Pokmon Scarlet and Violet's Ranked Battles Season 20 has begun, will run through to July 31st

 Posted on May 27, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  580 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon
Pokémon Scarlet and Violet's Ranked Battles Season 20 has now begun, and will run through to July 31st, at 11:59pm (UTC). This latest season continues under the Regulation Set G format which launched at the beginning of Season 18 in May.

Pokémon may be entered at any level, but will be set to level 50 during matches. Pokémon may also be given Held Items, however no more than one copy of any item can be present on a player's team. All battles will be limited to 20 minutes total, including 7 minutes of time per player, with Turn Time and Preview Times of 45 and 90 seconds respectively.

To start a Ranked Battle in Pokémon Scarlet or Violet, follow these steps in-game:

  • Launch your Pokémon Scarlet or Pokémon Violet game.
  • Open the X menu.
  • Select Poké Portal.
  • Select Battle Stadium.
  • Select Ranked Battles.
Season 20 Rewards
Players will be matched up with other players of similar rank to ensure even competition. Players will need to finish at least one match with a win or loss season to be eligible for rewards at the end of the season. The Season 20 rewards include the following:
  • Master Ball Tier
    • Sour Herba Mystica ×5
    • Dream Ball ×1
    • Gold Bottle Cap ×1
    • Bottle Cap ×3
    • Ability Patch ×1

    • Bottle Cap ×1

    • 150,000 League Points
  • Great Ball Tier

    • Bottle Cap ×1
    • Ability Capsule ×1
    • 60,000 League Points
  • Poké Ball Tier
    • TM171 (Tera Blast) ×1
    • 20,000 League Points
  • Beginner Tier
